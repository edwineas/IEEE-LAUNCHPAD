IEEE Launchpad Kerala Newsletter

Empowering your engineering career journey, IEEE Launchpad Kerala is back! This annual event, organized by the IEEE Kerala Section and GTech MuLearn, bridges the gap between aspiring engineers and industry leaders.

Our mission is to create a platform for students, graduates, and professionals to connect with potential employers, gain valuable career insights, and unlock exciting opportunities in the technical and engineering fields.

Join Us!

Get involved with IEEE Launchpad Kerala and take your career to the next level. Here's how you can participate:

  • Register as a participant: Gain access to exclusive job opportunities, interact with companies, and attend workshops conducted by industry experts.
  • Contribute as a partner: Showcase your brand and connect with top talent by sponsoring or participating in the event.

Latest Updates

  • Technical Challenge Deadline: Don't miss out! The deadline to start your Launchpad technical challenges is July 1st. Showcase your skills and earn valuable Karma points.
  • Doubt Clearance Sessions: We've got you covered! Join our doubt clearance sessions every Tuesday, Thursday, and Saturday from 8:30 PM to 10 PM IST for personalized support.

Event Roadmap

  • Registration & Community Building: Applications are now open! Join the GTech MuLearn Discord Server and connect with fellow participants.
  • Technical Challenges: Complete the challenges designed to assess your foundational and specialized skills. Earn recognition and climb the leaderboard.
  • Industry-curated Challenges & Hackathons: Get hands-on experience and impress potential employers with industry-relevant projects.
  • Job Fair & Career Guidance: Connect with leading companies from the technical and engineering sectors. Get valuable career advice from industry experts.
